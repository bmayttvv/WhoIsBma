Lesson 1 - adding text

To add text its farely simple, there are 2 ways to add text

H Tags

h tags stand for heading and they are used for titles, h6 is the smallest h1 is the biggest. To add h tags you can do
<h1> </h1>
<h2> </h2>
<h3> </h3>
<h4> </h4>
<h5> </h5>
<h6> </h6>

P Tags

p tag are simuler to h tags but there is only one and its used for paragraphs. To add a p tag doing
<p> </p>
